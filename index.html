Tomás Novo


My name is Tomás Novo. I'm 22 years old and I'm from Bragança, Portugal. Currently, I'm at the 4th year of Informatics Engineering at FEUP, Porto. The first programming language I've had contact with was Pascal at my high school, were I developed simple programs like a calculator.

Web Developer (2019)

In September of 2019, I worked as web developer for StartUp Buzz. I improved some frontend funcionalities of the web pages and updated information. With this project I learnt a lot about hosting.



Intern (2018)

In August of 2018, I did an Internship at Bragança's city hall, where I learnt about how things work at a city hall and where I installed software and hardware.

Disc Jockey (2017 - 2018)

In the summers of 2017 and 2018 I shared my musical taste as DJ by invite of the Bragança's city hall as part of the event "Verão Bragança - Viver a Cidade". My artistic name is DJ Corleone, due to a Godfather's joke with a friend of mine that I lost.




Volunteer (2017)

On December of 2017, I worked as volunteer for the Comic Con event that happenned in Portugal. My job was to support a photographer, asking for signatures for copyrights. I learned a lot about how hard it is to organize an event with so many people and about the burocracies around that organization.
